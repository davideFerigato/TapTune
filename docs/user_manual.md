# User Manual

## Getting started
1. Power the device via USB (5V).
2. You will hear a startup chime and the OLED will show "TapTune".
3. The RGB LED turns **red** – ready to pair.

## Pairing (NFC / button)
- Tap your NFC‑enabled phone against the NFC module (or press the button).  
  The OLED wakes up and displays a pulsing circle.
- Search for "TapTune" in your phone's Bluetooth settings and connect.
- A connection sound confirms pairing; LED turns **blue**.

## Playing music
- Start any audio app on your phone.  
  The OLED animates expanding rings, and the LED turns **green**.
- Use the physical volume knob on the amplifier to adjust loudness.

## Auto power‑off
- When disconnected, the display turns off after 20 seconds to save power.  
  The LED remains active (red) to show the device is on.
- Touch the NFC tag or press the button to wake the display again.

## Optional sensors
- **Photoresistor**: automatically adjusts display brightness.
- **PIR motion sensor**: wakes the display when you approach.
